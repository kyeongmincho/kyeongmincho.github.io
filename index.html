Kyeongmin Cho (조경민)

Ph.D. Candidate

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Concurrency and Parallelism Laboratory, KAIST School of Computing. I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Jeehoon Kang. Prior to KAIST, I received my B.S. in CSE and B.A. in Philosophy from Inha University.

My primary research interests lie in the area of concurrency, persistency and programming languages. In particular, I am interested in developing and verifying design patterns for coordinating concurrent accesses, and using the design patterns, building practical concurrent systems.

You can contact me on kyeongmin.cho@kaist.ac.kr.
